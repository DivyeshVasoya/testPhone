How can I see my past billing statements?

You can view your past billing statements anytime by logging into your Phone.com account.

  1. Log in to your account.
  2. Go to the Billing section from the main menu.
  3. Select Statements to see a list of your monthly invoices.
  4. Click on any statement to download or view the PDF version.

If you need a copy sent via email, please contact our support team.
