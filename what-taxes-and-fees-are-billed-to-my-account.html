What taxes and fees are billed to my account?

Your bill may include government-mandated taxes and regulatory fees, such as:

  • Federal Universal Service Fund (USF) – supports nationwide telecom services.
  • State and Local Taxes – may vary depending on your location.
  • 911 Emergency Service Fees – required to support emergency call routing.
  • Regulatory Recovery Fees – help cover compliance costs.

Exact charges will be itemized on your monthly statement.
